About Weeping Tiger

At Weeping Tiger, we believe that everyone deserves a soft friend to hold onto during tough times. Our plushies are designed to bring comfort, warmth, and emotional support when you need it most.

Each Weeping Tiger plush is crafted with care to remind you that it’s okay to feel sad sometimes — and that you don’t have to face those moments alone. Whether it’s stress from school, work, or life, a Weeping Tiger plush is here to sit quietly with you until the storm passes.
